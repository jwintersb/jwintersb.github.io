Joseph Winters

Environmental journalist

I'm a climate reporter covering plastics, corporate sustainability pledges, and environmental justice, among other topics. I'm currently researching the rights-of-nature movement at the University of Cambridge. Previously, I was a staff writer at Grist. I have reported on the U.N. plastics treaty negotiations; loopholes in international waste law; a waste picker collective in Portland, Oregon; the unraveling of U.S. climate policy, and more. When I'm not working, I'm probably running or cross-country skiing. Please don't hesitate to reach out.
