Joseph Winters

Reporter covering plastics, greenwashing, and corporate climate accountability, among other topics.

I'm a staff writer at Grist. Much of my reporting focuses on waste, especially plastics, recycling, and the idea of a "circular economy." But I have many other interests too, and I've reported on everything from carbon markets to veganism to the rights of nature.
